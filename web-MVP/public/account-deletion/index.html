Last updated: June 4, 2026

This page explains how users of Ủ - Chăm sóc sức khỏe can request deletion of their account and related data.

Delete Your Account In The App

  1. Open the Ủ app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open account/settings actions and choose Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Request Deletion By Email

If you cannot access the app, send an account deletion request to uchamsocsuckhoe88@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Ủ account. Please include the subject line: Account deletion request - Ủ Health.

Data Deleted

When an account deletion request is completed, we delete or anonymize account-related data such as:

  • account profile information, including name and email address;
  • health profile information, including age, height, weight, goal, and BMI-related values;
  • meal logs, AI scan history, food images, water logs, workout activity, reminders, and app ratings;
  • authentication tokens and app session data.

Data Retained

We may retain limited records when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or backup recovery. These records are kept only as long as reasonably necessary for those purposes. Backup copies may take up to 90 days to be fully removed from backup systems.

Partial Data Requests

Users may also request deletion of specific data without deleting the full account by contacting uchamsocsuckhoe88@gmail.com. For example, users may request removal of meal logs, food scan images, or profile information where deletion is technically and legally possible.
